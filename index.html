Face Detection And Recognition

A system that detects and recognises faces in real-time videos.

This system helps us recognise people in real time videos. We are working on to enhance the accuracy in terms of identifying or verifying faces.We are using an Image based approach using the method of neural network that gives a very high performance, it is faster and more accurate in comparison to other feature based approaches, We have tried to incorporate multiview face recognition that strives to detect faces that are out of the plane rotation.

We have utilized multiple Digital Image Processing techniques , Machine learning algorithms along with layers like Convo2D, Maxpool, Dropout, Dense, Flatten to develope the model. We later trained it to the best of our ability and there was a gradual increase in accuracy.Over the course of multiple training we obtained an accuracy of 60% and after cleaning the dataset and increasing number of epochs we were able to stretch our accuracy to 90%.
